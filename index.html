Our Story

Palm & Pine began with a simple mission: to create skincare products that are good for you and good for the planet.

Founded in 2018 by botanist Elena Foster, our company focuses on sustainable sourcing from palm and pine forests that are responsibly managed. We believe that beauty should never come at the expense of our environment.

Every product is carefully crafted in small batches using traditional methods combined with modern science to ensure effectiveness and purity.
